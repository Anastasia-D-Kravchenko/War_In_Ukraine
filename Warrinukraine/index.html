VOLUNTEERS

Thank you!

We want to live in a successful country. And the country is defined by people. When everyone cares about their surroundings and solves the problems that bother them, the country develops.

Volunteers are ordinary people who invest their time, talent or resources in implementing socially useful projects. Everyone can do good deeds: children, adults, people with increased needs, companies, agencies and even entire countries.

Even one good deed can change society, the country and the world for the better.

Today, volunteer organizations exist in 80 countries of the world. About 110 million people annually participate in voluntary programs, where they help in the fields of education, health care, ecology, social protection and many others.

Together to victory!

The Armed Forces of Ukraine is a military formation that, according to the Constitution of Ukraine, is responsible for the defense of Ukraine, the protection of its sovereignty, territorial integrity and inviolability.

The Armed Forces of Ukraine ensure deterrence of armed aggression against Ukraine and repulse it, protection of the state's airspace and underwater space within the territorial sea of ​​Ukraine.

Unions, military units and units of the Armed Forces of Ukraine, in accordance with the law, within the limits determined by decrees of the President of Ukraine, approved by the Verkhovna Rada of Ukraine, may be involved in the implementation of measures of the legal regime of martial law and state of emergency, strengthening the protection of the state border of Ukraine and the exclusive (maritime) ) the economic zone, the continental shelf of Ukraine and their legal registration, the liquidation of natural and man-made emergencies, the provision of military assistance to other states, as well as to participate in international military cooperation and international peacekeeping operations on the basis of international treaties of Ukraine and in the order and conditions, defined by the legislation of Ukraine.

Come back alive

This fund has been actively operating since 2014, so on the eve of the full-scale invasion, concerned Ukrainians sent 48 million hryvnias to its account.

Since then, donating to the needs of the army has become a traditional ritual after receiving a salary. Everyone who cares actively participates in the meetings of this fund.

So, at the end of July, the total amount of Ukrainian national aid to our army amounted to UAH 4 billion. And this is just a check from one of the funds.

Activities of the Povrnys zhivym fund The main activity of the fund is the purchase of tactical equipment for our soldiers: thermal imagers, protective equipment, quadcopters and cars, reconnaissance and fire countermeasures.

Therefore, the following have already appeared in the service of the soldiers of the Armed Forces of Ukraine:

Bayraktar TB2 with three strike drones, 25 Leka-100 unmanned complexes, 20 PD-2 complexes, which include 20 drones and ground stations for them.

Shelters

Some of the most famous initiatives of Prytula were: a charity auction together with the Kalush Orchestra, where funds were collected for the needs of the Armed Forces of Ukraine and commenting on the match with Skichk, during which they collected for tactical tablets for the military.

And the loudest collection of funds from the Prytula fund was the collection for the legendary Bayraktar drones. A Turkish company decided to help Ukrainians and gave them as a gift, so public money went to a satellite from the ICEYE company.

Activities of the Prytula Foundation In general, this foundation provides Ukrainian soldiers with means of communication, optical devices, drones and UAVs, transport and means of tactical medicine.

The fund does not forget about the injured civilians. Part of the funds are directed to the frontline humanitarian headquarters, which helps civilians in occupation and frontline towns and villages.

We will win!

8 ways to help Ukraine in the rear:

  1. Financial assistance to the Ukrainian army

    2. In two clicks you can support the Ukrainian army! You can donate money to the needs of the Ukrainian army through the "Diya" application, PrivatBank and monobank, and you can also support proven charitable funds, in particular, "Return Alive" and the Serhiy Prytula Foundation. Details for charitable contributions at the link.

  2. Assistance in humanitarian aid headquarters.

    3. Currently, there are many humanitarian headquarters all over Ukraine that collect provisions and ammunition for the military: food, clothing, hygiene products, body armor, etc. Every day, such headquarters receive a large number of items. You can help coordinate the process, sort medicines or clothes. Or bring what you can give to the humanitarian headquarters for the needs of the army or the displaced. Read more about humanitarian aid at the link.

  3. Assistance to displaced persons.

    4. Many people have left their homes because of the enemy who is trying to destroy our cities. These people took only documents and the most necessary things with them. Currently, most of the displaced people spend the night in special shelters, where they are provided with food, clothing and medical care. Depending on your skills, you can help such headquarters function continuously: sorting products and clothes, cooking, for people with a medical education - on duty at locations and providing medical assistance.

  4. Assistance with equipment and clothing for defenders.

    5. In many cities, there are workshops for sewing warm clothes, weaving camouflage nets, etc. Such shops need materials and volunteers who are ready to help. Find locations in your city and call the organizers directly to find out what kind of help is needed.

  5. Automotive volunteering

    6. In addition to "hands-on" help, many headquarters are also looking for volunteers with cars. Usually the task is to deliver necessary things, products or ammunition. Or transportation of displaced persons from local train stations to organized places of overnight stay. Register as a car volunteer in the UBS chatbot using the link.

  6. Online volunteering and the IT army of Ukraine

    7. Cyber ​​volunteering is no less important than the previous ones. Blocking enemy sites and disseminating truthful information is a separate and important front in wartime. You can join the IT army and help remotely using this link. In addition, you can join organizations, charitable foundations and initiatives that are currently actively helping. Copywriting, design, editing, etc., these skills are valued and especially important now.

  7. Territorial defense of the city

    8. If you want to help directly in the defense of your city, join the local territorial defense headquarters. Many saboteurs have appeared in our cities, who want to disturb the peace of local citizens. Teroborona protects civilians from enemies, patrols cities and protects our sleep. You can join the TRO in your city by following the link.
